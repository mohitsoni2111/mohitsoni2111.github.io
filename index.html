About Me

Hey there! I'm Mohit, from Delhi, India. I am a master's student at NC State University in the Computer Science Department with a focus in Machine Learning and Data Science.

As a child, I was always captivated by the mystery and complexity of the machines that surrounded me. My family's first computer was a clunky, beige box, and although it was far from cutting-edge, I was enchanted by the idea of controlling it with a series of typed commands. It was my gateway into a digital world filled with endless possibilities.

In high school, I had a transformative moment. I stumbled upon an online course on AI and machine learning. The course was a revelation, like unlocking the secrets of a magical world hidden within the computer's depths. I was spellbound by the concept of teaching machines to learn and make decisions on their own. It was like having a conversation with the future.

The more I delved into the world of AI and machine learning, the more I realized the profound impact it could have on our lives. I saw how these technologies were revolutionizing industries, from healthcare to finance, and from transportation to entertainment. The potential to solve complex problems, automate repetitive tasks, and even understand human language through NLP was awe-inspiring.

My passion for this field deepened as I started working on projects that applied machine learning and NLP to real-world problems.

Today, my passion for computer science, machine learning, AI, and NLP continues to drive me forward. I see these fields as a way to shape a brighter future, where intelligent machines collaborate with humans to solve the world's most pressing challenges. Whether it's deciphering languages, understanding emotions, or creating algorithms that can make the world a better place, I am committed to this journey of discovery and innovation.

I am also learning Statistics and occasionally solve problems on Leetcode.

Apart from this, I enjoy watching movies and anime. I love to watch different sports and I am a fitness enthusiast.
